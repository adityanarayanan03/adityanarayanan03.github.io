One of my non-academic passions is violin. I started playing violin with my teacher of 12 years, Ms. Diedra Lawrence, when I was 5, and I joined my district orchestra program in fifth grade. I love peforming both solo and with my high school orchestra. I won the 2018 [Brazos Valley Concerto Competition](https://tinyurl.com/Aditya-BVSO-TheEagle), and performed in Carnegie Hall with my school orchestra in 2019. Some of my favorite works that I've performed include Massenet's "Meditation" from the opera Thais, Kreisler's "Praeludium and Allegro", and Vaughan Williams's "Fantasia on a theme by Thomas Tallis".
