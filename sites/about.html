TLDR: I'm a Freshman at the University of Texas at Austin studying Electrical and Computer Engineering. I'm a problem solver that loves building and making and my dream is to be a robotics engineer, building robots that work with humans for society's benefit.

The full version: My name is Aditya Narayanan and I'm studying electrical and computer engineering (ECE) at UT Austin. I'm originally from College Station, Texas, and I graduated from College Station High School in 2021. I've always been a maker, and that's because being a maker is all about solving problems in creative ways. I got into competition robotics in high school, as designing and building complex robotic systems to solve new and interesting problems was absolutely thrilling. In college, I love attending hackathons to replicate the thrill I felt competing in robotics in high school. During high school, I spent every summer in Electrical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering research labs at Texas A&M University, where I got to apply my engineering problem-solving skills to real world, modern problems. I currently work in the Swarm Robotics Lab at UT Austin, where I research computer vision on single board computers.
